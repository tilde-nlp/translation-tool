About Translate 2015

Translate 2015 can be used to easily translate texts, full documents, and websites from Latvian into English and from English into Latvian.

Users can follow events in the Latvian media, translate press releases and other information, read local news sites and blogs, and access government documents for general research purposes.

This translation tool was specially designed for the Presidency of the Council of the European Union, providing quick access to information for international journalists, delegates, and other visitors to Latvia in 2015.

Translate 2015 is powered by Hugo, a machine translation (MT) system created for the Latvian government. The system is integrated into e-gov platforms, websites, and registries, enabling multilingual communication in the public sector. This helps ensure that all Latvian residents can access information and receive e-services in their native language.

The machine translation technology in Hugo and Translate 2015 was developed by Tilde, a language technology company based in Riga, Tallinn, and Vilnius.
