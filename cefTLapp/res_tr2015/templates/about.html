About Translate 2015

Translate 2015 can be used to instantly translate texts, documents, and websites from Latvian into English and vice versa.

With Translate 2015, users can follow events in the Latvian media, read local websites and blogs, translate press information, and access government documents for general research purposes.

This translation tool was specially designed for the Presidency of the Council of the European Union, providing quick access to information for journalists, delegates, and other visitors to Latvia in 2015.

Translate 2015 is powered by Hugo, a machine translation system created for the Latvian government. The system is integrated into e-gov platforms and websites, enabling multilingual communication in the public sector. This allows users to access information and receive e-services in multiple languages.

Hugo is Latvia’s contribution to Europe’s automated translation infrastructure (currently in development). Together with MT@EC and other national projects, this automated translation infrastructure will help to crack the language barrier between the nations of Europe.

The machine translation technology in Hugo and Translate 2015 was developed by Tilde, a language technology company based in Riga, Tallinn, and Vilnius.
