Machine translation

 

Machine translation, or MT, allows you to translate words, phrases, and even entire documents with the click of a button.

 

The technology helps give readers a general sense of a text’s content. MT is ideally suited for tasks like technical translation, data analysis, media monitoring, and market research.

 

MT systems can also be customized for a particular company or organization, taking into account its specific terminology and style. Systems can then be integrated into an organization’s platforms and applications.

 

By promoting multilingual communication, MT bridges language barriers between nations, cultures, and people.
